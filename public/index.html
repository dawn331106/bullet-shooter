How To Play Bullet Shooter

Goal

Reduce your opponent's health from 3 to 0 before they do the same to you.

Bullets bounce off walls, so bank shots are a key strategy.

Controls

Move: WASD or Arrow Keys

Aim: Mouse cursor

Shoot: Left mouse click

Game Flow

1. Choose Play vs Bot for solo practice or create/join an online room for PvP.

2. In online mode, the host creates a room and shares the 6-letter room code.

3. Both players enter the lobby. The host presses Start Match.

4. A short countdown begins, then the round starts.

Rules

Each player starts with 3 health.

If you have a shield, the next hit removes the shield instead of health.

The max active bullet capacity increases over time, up to 7.

If you collect +1 Bullet, you can fire one extra shot even when at bullet limit.

Boosters

+10% / +20% / +30% speed: Increases your bullet speed.

+5% / +10% move: Increases your movement speed.

Shield: Blocks one incoming hit.

+1 Bullet: Grants one extra bullet shot beyond current cap.

Winning

You win the round when your opponent reaches 0 health.

After a round, both players can choose Rematch or return to menu.
